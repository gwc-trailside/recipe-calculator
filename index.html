Fruit Popsicles

Servings-8

Serving Quantity:

Ingredients

2 cups raspberries
2 cups plain or vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup white sugar
8 small paper cups
8 popsicle sticks

Place the raspberries, yogurt, and sugar into a blender. Cover, and blend until fruit is chunky or smooth, as desired.

Then fill paper cups 3/4 full with fruit mixture. Cover the top of each cup with a strip of aluminum foil. Poke a popsicle stick through the center of the foil of each cup.

Place the cups in the freezer for at least 5 hours. To serve, remove foil and peel off the paper cup.
